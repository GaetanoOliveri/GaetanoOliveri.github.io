About me

My name is Gaetano Oliveri and I am a Sophomore at Virginia Tech University in Blacksburg, VA. I am currently a chemical engineering student with a GPA of 3.3. My primary interest is in an engineering assignment that will allow me to demonstrate and apply my learnings from my curriculum as well as my leadership and core skills that I have developed in athletics and the building trades. Throughout my life I have always been fascinated with problem solving and the corresponding satisfaction in developing and building solutions. For example, during the summers of 2020 and 2021 I was given the opportunity to work with a construction crew that built foundations for homes. This allowed me to begin to see practical engineering in a real setting while also seeing the fruits of my hard work realized. While in high school I was part of the robotics team where I saw first hand the aspects of design and development as part of that team. I enjoyed thinking about the design of our robot and the challenge of taking that design and creating a working bot. That was really both a challenge and a lot of fun! Finally, I was also the co-captain of our swim team where I had the chance to lead and motivate my teammates in competition. Thank you for your time and consideration.
